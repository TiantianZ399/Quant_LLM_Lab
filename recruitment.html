Recruitment

We recruit students who are excited about the intersection of large language models, quantitative research, and technical building.

Who We Are Looking For

Curiosity. Passion. Resilience.

We care about seriousness, collaboration, and growth more than polished credentials.

Quantitative researchers

Students who enjoy math, statistics, markets, modeling, probability, optimization, and careful empirical thinking.

LLM engineers

Students who want to build systems around models: agents, evals, retrieval, data pipelines, interfaces, and infrastructure.

AI researchers / scientists

Students who want to read papers deeply, ask original questions, run experiments, and build a portfolio of research taste.
